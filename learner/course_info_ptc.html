Driving NC II

Driving NC II is a short TESDA course in the Philippines that will train you in operating light motor vehicles classified under LTO Restriction code 1 and 2, transporting passengers and loads over specified routes to local or district location, and performing minor vehicle repairs and other minor servicing.

PV Systems Installation NC II is a short TESDA course in the Philippines that will train you in performing site assessment, checking PV components and materials' compliance, installing and commissioning PV system and preparing documentation requirements for PV systems installation.
